A calm wedding morning rarely happens by accident. It usually comes from a realistic hair and make-up schedule that leaves room for delays, dressing time, and a few quiet moments before you leave.

Ask your artist how long each service actually takes, then build in buffer time. The order matters too. Think about who needs to be ready first for photographs, travel, or childcare.

Trials are useful because they test more than the look. They help you confirm timings, product choices, and whether the artist understands how you want to feel as well as how you want to look.
