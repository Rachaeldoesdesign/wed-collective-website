A venue shapes far more than the backdrop. It affects your ceremony timings, catering setup, guest travel, and how relaxed the day feels once everyone arrives.

Start with the non-negotiables: capacity, location, accessibility, and whether the space suits the style of celebration you want. A beautiful room stops being useful very quickly if it cannot comfortably hold your guests or support the running order you have in mind.

When you visit, ask what is included in the price, where suppliers can set up, how long you have for access, and what the wet-weather plan looks like. Those details usually tell you more than the brochure does.
