Flowers do more than decorate tables. They help set the tone of the day, shape your palette, and soften a venue in all the right places.

Starting with the season gives your florist more room to create something that feels natural and considered. It can also be a better use of budget than insisting on specific blooms that are harder to source well.

Share reference images for mood and scale, then let your florist guide the exact stems. You will usually get a stronger result when the brief is clear but not over-controlled.
