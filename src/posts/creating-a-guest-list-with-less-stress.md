The guest list affects almost every other decision, from venue options to catering costs, so it is worth settling your approach early.

Start with the people you cannot imagine the day without. Then look at family expectations, work out where you both agree, and be honest about the total number the budget can support.

If difficult conversations are likely, it helps to stay consistent. A clear rule is easier to explain than a series of exceptions made under pressure.
