The final details often arrive late in the process, which is why they can feel more chaotic than they need to. A simple checklist helps you catch what still needs wording, printing, packing, or confirming.

Think about every item guests interact with: place cards, table plans, welcome signage, order of the day, and menus. If the tone and information are consistent, the whole celebration feels more considered.

This is also the moment to gather supplier contacts, delivery notes, and setup instructions in one place. A tidy handover can make the final week far calmer.
