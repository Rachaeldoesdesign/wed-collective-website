Photography is one of the few parts of your wedding you will return to for years, so the booking should be about more than price and Instagram highlights.

Ask to see full galleries rather than only a handful of portfolio images. That gives you a clearer sense of how they handle dark ceremony spaces, family group shots, weather changes, and evening receptions.

It also helps to understand their working style. Some couples want gentle direction, while others prefer a documentary approach. The right answer is the one that suits how you want the day to feel.
