Budget conversations get easier once you decide what matters most. For some couples that is the venue, for others it is food, photography, or a brilliant party at the end of the night.

Set a total figure, split it into broad categories, and keep a small contingency aside from the start. That buffer helps with delivery fees, extra guests, and the little upgrades that appear once decisions are underway.

The goal is not to track every penny obsessively. It is to make clear choices early enough that your spending still feels intentional later on.
